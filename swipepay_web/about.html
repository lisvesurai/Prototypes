About Us

Based in Alexandria, Virginia with offices in Seattle, SwipePay Mobile, Inc. was founded in 2005 with the objective of solving the billing and payment needs of the prepaid operator. SwipePay is currently being managed by a team that collectively has over 30-years of marketing, technology and wireless experience. Their backgrounds include experience in both start-ups and Fortune 500 Companies including Sprint, Boost Mobile, Sybase 365 (InphoMatch/Mobile 365), Qwest, Intuit, Ztango (WiderThan) and several other successful ventures. SwipePay Mobile's management is complemented by employees and advisors with strong consumer marketing and business development experience both internationally and domestically.
