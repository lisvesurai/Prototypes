Products/Services

Mobile Operators

SwipePay provides secure funds transfer across prepaid operators, mobile developers and messaging aggregators enabling them to maximize data and voice revenue while reducing operating and high transaction failure rates. SwipePay is designed as both a secure Java/Brew application, a WAP service extension and is available as a set of API's that support critical funds transfer activities across postpaid and prepaid mobile operators.

Mobile Application Developers and Messaging Aggregators

SwipePay Mobile is also focused on providing funds transfer services across its proprietary platforms via third party WAP and Java applications. Our service enables mobile prepaid topup and funds gifting from 3rd party applications, thus ensuring higher transaction rates, lower failures, and higher ARPU across all distribution points.

Service Offering:

  • Funds Brokering
  • Prepaid Reload and Gifting Interoperability
  • Overdraft Spending
  • Global P2P Funds Transfer
  • Debit Card Issuance and Loading
