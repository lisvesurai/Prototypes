What is SwipePay?

SwipePay Mobile is a next generation solution in mobile payments. We focus on the problems that plague mobile prepaid companies such as high reload costs and failure rates associated with subscription products and premium SMS.

Our product offering consists of a secure mobile phone application and middleware platform that facilitates prepaid reload and gifting interoperability across prepaid mobile operators. SwipePay works with debit and credit card processors, banks, mobile operators, mobile publishers and premium messaging aggregators so their customers have the power to access, transfer and manage funds without ever needing a physical banking presence or traditional Internet connection. The primary objective is to reduce load costs and maximize revenue for the operators while bringing convenience, speed and simplicity to the way mobile subscribers load and transfer funds.

SwipePay Mobile is changing the prepaid mobile paradigm with the next generation in mobile prepaid payments. Harnessing the power of our global network, SwipePay has set out to breakdown the payment barriers across operators with the goals to unify the global prepaid marketplace reload infrastructure, reduce carrier operational costs, and add convenience to the reload process for all prepaid mobile subscribers.
