Why SwipePay

Several problems are occurring in the prepaid mobile industry, specifically around billing, reload and operating costs. These problems not only impact the Mobile Operator, but each member of the value chain in delivering premium services.  SwipePay was created to solve these problems by deploying targeted solutions that positively impact ARPU and decrease operating costs for each member within the value chain.

Key Problems Facing Prepaid Operators and Mobile Publishers:

  1. 40 percent failure rates for service transactions.
  2. 30 percent of the prepaid mobile audience at any given time carries a $0 balance in their prepaid wireless accounts.
  3. Data services and premium messaging continues to grow while publishers and operators are failing to maximize subscriber revenue.
  4. Reload costs are as high as $7.50 per reload due to existing processes and commission structures.
  5. Carriers have failed to capture revenue from P2P transactions despite their perfect position to service the expanding unbanked/underbanked market

As a result of our direct experience in the prepaid industry, we know first hand of the challenges facing Mobile Operators and Developers. Our experts are deeply knowledgeable of prepaid billing systems, content delivery platforms, transaction processing, m-commerce and premium SMS. With key technologies in place, SwipePay integrates into the carrier networks and the global financial marketplace to facilitate cost effective, cross carrier mobile transactions, bringing a truly unique offering to the international mobile community.
